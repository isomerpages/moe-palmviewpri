<h2>ART CLUB</h2>
<p>The art club aims to develop visual literate students, they will be able
to observe, understand and make meaning of what they see. They will be
able to communicate their ideas by using and creating visuals. We want
them to be able to see the value and recognise the relevance of art in
their lives.</p>
<p>Students will be exposed to various art forms and mediums such as drawing,
painting, mixed media, clay and craft. Through the inquiry based learning
process, students will gain a better understanding of art concepts and
ideas.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students will be exposed to different art styles, artists and art movements
through visits to art galleries and museums.</p>
<h5>Learning Outcomes</h5>
<p><strong><em>Overall</em></strong> 
<br>-To motivate continual self-practice and further exploration
<br>-To acquire skills, language and confidence to identify valuable exploration
<br>-To experience personal expression through process and actions instead
of polished artwork
<br>-To experience the setting up an exhibition (curating process)</p>
<p><strong><em>2D Art</em></strong> 
<br>-To increase the competency of drawing through the development of observational
skills</p>
<p><strong><em>Painting</em></strong>
<br>-To apply various painting techniques appropriately and effectively
<br>-To have competent knowledge of judging colour temperature and mixing
of colours
<br>-To control and tweak various elements at each stage of the painting process</p>
<p><strong><em>3D Art</em></strong>
<br>-To explore the relationship between 2D and 3D artworks
<br>-To create 3D sculptures using simple materials placing emphasis on the
creative processes
<br>-To investigate formal, functional, and conceptual issues while developing
effective material choices, construction methods, craftsmanship, and safe
studio working habits.
<br>-To consider issues such as sustainable materials and reuse, and how objects
function in the world.&nbsp;
<br>-To have a better understanding of materials and better control over the
outcomes</p>
