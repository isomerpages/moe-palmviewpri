The main objective of the Malay Language Department is to instill creativity and confidence among the students by means of effective classroom engagement so as to facilitate them being effective learners and confident users of the language. In addition, with the introduction of the current Malay Language Syllabus, CEKAP, the department also aims at developing one’s interactive and communication skills to inspire confidence amongst the students.

<img src="/images/ML Website.png" style="width:90%">

### **Signature Programmes:**

**Mother Tongue Fortnight (Malay)** <br>
The Mother Tongue Fortnight is a two-week long enrichment programme that is specifically aimed at promoting as well as exposing the students to the various aspects of the Malay Culture. Through various activities such as the wayang kulit, makan berdulang, kuih ondeh-ondeh making, as well as learning the renowned Malay martial arts, Silat, students were actively engaged throughout this period.
