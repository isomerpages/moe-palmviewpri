<h2>LEARNING FOR LIFE PROGRAMME</h2>
<h6>Palm View Challenge (PVC) – A Leader, An Advocate, A Force For Good</h6>
<p>Palm View Challenge is a 6-year school-wide programme designed in line
with different learning experiences aimed at developing children’s life
skills, values and social emotional competencies. Students at each level
are challenged to complete different tasks, each carefully designed to
develop in them desirable character traits for the 21st century. By the
end of our students’ 6-year journey with the school, we aim to provide
them with a holistic development in the cognitive, physical, social and
moral domains.</p>
